How To Use Plants In Interior Design

Plant Care Guide

  • Ensure the plant receives adequate sunlight each day.
  • Water the plant regularly, but avoid overwatering to prevent root rot.
  • Use well-draining soil to promote healthy root growth.
  • Fertilize the plant monthly during the growing season.
  • Monitor for pests and diseases, and take appropriate action if detected.
  • Prune the plant as needed to maintain its shape and encourage new growth.
  • Rotate the plant occasionally to ensure even growth on all sides.
  • Provide adequate humidity for plants that require it, especially during winter months.
  • Avoid placing plants near drafts or heating vents to prevent stress.
  • Repot the plant when it outgrows its current container or every 1-2 years.
  • Research the specific care needs of your plant species for best results.
